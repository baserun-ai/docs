**[Baserun](https://baserun.ai)** is the testing and observability platform for LLM apps.

## Features

- [Testing](/testing) - test your LLM features as easily as writing a unit test.
- [Evaluation](/evaluation) - evaluate outputs using builtin or custom metrics.
- [Monitoring](/tracing) - record and observe traces of your app in production.
